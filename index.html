About Us

At Mug & Munch, we take pride in offering an authentic Sri Lankan coffee experience combined with freshly baked pastries made from the finest ingredients. With three welcoming branches located in Colombo, Kandy, and the breathtaking surroundings of Ambuluwawa, we have become a favorite destination for coffee enthusiasts and food lovers alike.

What We Serve

Our coffee is brewed from premium, locally sourced beans, capturing the rich and bold flavors unique to Sri Lanka. Our pastries are crafted daily using fresh ingredients to ensure a delightful culinary experience for every guest.
